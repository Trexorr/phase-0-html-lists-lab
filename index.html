Tristens Grilled Cheese Supreme!

Ingredients:

  • Slices of bread x 2
  • Slices of cheese x 3
    • Chedder
    • Mozzerella
    • Pepper Jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Cooking instructions

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread on frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
